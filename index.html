Hi, I'm Aishwarya. I am an Applied Scientist at Alexa AI, Amazon and I work on conversational AI research. Previously, I completed my PhD in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin. My research with Prof. Ray Mooney was aimed at enabling lifelong learning in grounded language understanding systems, with a focus on human-robot dialog applications.

I completed my B.Tech. (Hons) in May 2015 in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, Chennai, India. I was brought up in Bangalore, India and completed my schooling at National Public School, Indiranagar.
